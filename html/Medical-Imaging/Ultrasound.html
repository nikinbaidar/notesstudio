Properties of US Beam

The area through which hte sound energy is emitted from the US transducer travels is the US beam. US are sound waves whose frequency is \(> 2000 Hz\).

Beam Shape

  • The beam is three dimesional and is symmetrical around its central axis.
  • It can be divided into two regions: a near field (or freshnel zone) which is cylindrical in shape and a far field (or fraunhofer zone) where it diverges and becomes cone-shaped.
  • The actual shape of the beam depends on the diameter of the crystal, the frequency and wavelength and the design of the transducer.

Beam Intensity

The intensity of the beam is the power flowing through a unit are. The intenisty throughout the beam's length or width is not uniform.
